NX-1

A nimble light-jet concept built for efficient regional travel—fast turns, tight schedules, and high utilization.

Light Jet Agile Regional

Overview

The NX-1 is built around speed and simplicity—ideal for organizations that fly frequently and value flexibility. It’s designed as a compact platform that prioritizes efficiency without sacrificing comfort.

Use Cases

  • Regional business travel for small teams (2–6 passengers)
  • Short-notice trips and rapid scheduling changes
  • Multi-stop day routes (meetings, site visits, inspections)
  • High-frequency “out-and-back” missions
